Our Story

Making financial literacy accessible, one habit at a time

MoneyBuddy started in a classroom with a simple mission: to make financial literacy accessible to everyone. We don't believe in boring spreadsheets. We believe in visual habits and absolute privacy.

Your Data, Your PC
